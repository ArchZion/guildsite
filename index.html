About us

We are a newly formed guild of old WoW players.

We consist mainly of South African players who are all friends.

If you are a South African player who would just like to join a casual local guild, this is the place for you

Currently we are planning on doing all content.

We have dedicated players who do PVP and PVE

We aim for a friendly enviroment with jokes and laughter but with high focus and one common goal: To enjoy the game!

English/Afrikaans speaking
